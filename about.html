About Me

The journey of a builder, dreamer, and innovator.

"With 12+ years in software engineering and product management, I’m now diving deep into the world of AI while completing my Master’s in the field. This blog is my space to document my journey — from exploring NLP, LLMs, Recommendation Systems, and Reinforcement Learning to sharing insights, projects, and practical takes on AI research.

Along the way, I’ve co-authored a research paper on predicting emergency department readmissions and discovered a passion for aligning AI solutions with real-world needs. Whether it’s unraveling the intricacies of research papers, building innovative systems, or connecting theory to practice, I aim to share it all here.

If you're curious about AI’s possibilities and love digging into the 'why' and 'how,' let’s explore together!"
